Brand Concept

Dove's rebranding highlights a modern, fresh aesthetic while preserving the brand's signature authenticity and self-assurance. The brand message which encourages genuine beauty and self-acceptance hasn’t changed, but the packaging has been updated with simple, elegant designs that reflect simplicity and elegance. The goal of this new visual identity is to appeal to a wider demographic and further emphasize Dove’s mission of enabling people to feel good about themselves. The new packaging serves as a constant reminder that although outward looks can change, the brand's essential principles of inclusivity, confidence, and care never go.
